THE CHALLENGE

⚡

Redesign Reality

Participants must design and develop a fully functional, visually stunning webpage for the Xinity Hackathon Community and get an opportunity to be featured live on the official Xinity website.

🛠️

AI Augmented

AI tools are officially allowed for design, ideation, and development. Push the absolute boundaries of what's possible.

🏆

Glory & Recognition

Winners secure an official spot as a Xinity Tech Team Member, live website feature, and community-wide recognition.

EVENT TIMELINE

📥 Download Template

Round 2: Offline Showcase

Selected teams must present a fully functional, working webpage to the judges offline.

RULES & LIMITATIONS

📜

Eligibility & Teams

  • Open to solo individuals or teams of maximum 3 members.
  • All participants must be currently enrolled college students.
  • Inter-college and inter-specialization teams are allowed.
  • A participant cannot register in more than one team. Team composition is locked post-registration.
⚠️

Limitations

  • All work must be 100% original at its core logic. Plagiarism implies disqualification.
  • Late submissions will not be accepted.
🤖

AI & Tooling

  • AI tools are permitted for design, ideation, and development.
  • We encourage pushing the boundaries with AI.
