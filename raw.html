ABOUT THE EVENT

WebX Challenge is an exciting hackathon where creative minds come together to design and develop the official Xinity Hackathon Community webpage. This is your chance to leave a permanent mark on the Xinity platform.

THE CHALLENGE

Participants must design and develop a fully functional, visually stunning webpage for the Xinity Hackathon Community and get an opportunity to be featured live on the official Xinity website.

RULES

ROUND 1

[ONLINE]

2-3 APRIL 2026

Submit your idea in the official Xinity Template ppt provided.

ROUND 2

[OFFLINE]

4 APRIL 2026

Present your creative fully functional webpage.

  • Participation is open to solo individuals or teams of maximum 3 members.
  • All participants must be currently enrolled college students.
  • Inter-college and inter-specialization teams are allowed.
  • One member must be designated as the Team Lead for all communication.
  • A participant cannot register in more than one team.
  • Team composition cannot be changed after registration is confirmed.
  • Round 1: Submit your redesign concept using the official XINITY PPT Template in PDF format before the deadline.
  • Round 2: Selected teams must present a fully functional, working webpage to the judges offline.
  • All team members must be present on the day of Round 2.
  • Late submissions will not be accepted under any circumstances.
  • AI tools are officially allowed for design, ideation, and development.
  • All submitted work must be 100% original plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Judges' decisions are final and binding.
  • Respectful behaviour toward all judges, organizers, and participants is mandatory at all times.
